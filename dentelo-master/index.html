What We Provide

  • Electrotherapy

    metus e Electrotherapy is the use of electrical energy as a medical treatment. It is a non-invasive and painless treatment.

  • Hydrotherapy

    Hydrotherapy, water therapy, is a form of complementary and alternative medicine (CAM) that uses water for pain relief and treatment.

  • Gait Rehabilitation

    Gait rehabilitation is a type of physical therapy that helps people improve their walking ability.

    Burn healing is a complex process the body's natural ability to repair and regenerate damaged tissue.
